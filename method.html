Together, We Create!

Our Brand Consultations are based on 2 axes:



- The Business strategy
- The Marketing solutions

We first need to understand what is going on so we can tailor a customized solution for the needs of your company.

Defining your identity

A series of questions will give us the essential answers to move safely into the right direction. The questions will target your vision, beliefs and preferences. Once the form is completed, we schedule a 2nd meeting and go through each answer to have a clear picture of what we will be building.

The briefing

During this meeting, we will focus on strategy and marketing. This is a great opportunity to clear any doubts and talk about any questions you may have. After your validation and confirmation, Panam Design will start putting together moodboards that will be discussed during our 3rd meeting.

Sit back and relax

Our 3 moodboards are ready to be analyzed and discussed. We will present you 3 different environments based on the same briefing. Anything can be modeled, exchanged, replaced, and designed to your taste.
